About TravelGo

Our Mission

TravelGo helps adventurous travelers discover beautiful destinations around the world. We curate beach, temple, and country recommendations so you can plan trips with confidence.

Our Story

Founded in 2026 by a small team of full-stack developers and travel enthusiasts, TravelGo grew out of a final project for the IBM JavaScript Programming Essentials course. What started as a learning exercise turned into a hub for travel inspiration.

Our Team

  • Filipe Brito Ferreira - Founder & Full-Stack Developer
  • Travel Editorial Team - Curators of every recommendation
  • Community Contributors - Travelers who share their experiences

Our Values

  • Authentic recommendations from real travelers
  • Sustainable tourism and respect for local cultures
  • Open access to travel inspiration for everyone
